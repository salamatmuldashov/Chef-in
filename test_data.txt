Recipe:
Name: Caprese Salad
Image: https://natashaskitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/Caprese-Salad-6.jpg
Description: Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish made with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. It is typically served as a starter or as a side dish and is perfect for summertime when tomatoes are at their peak. The salad is simple, light, and refreshing, making it a perfect addition to any meal.
Steps: Slice fresh tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into even pieces.
Arrange tomato and cheese slices on a plate in an alternating pattern.
Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
Sprinkle salt and freshly ground black pepper over the salad.
Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
